Design Notes: Finding Your Blueprint Style

Why offer multiple formats? Different projects need different visualization strategies. The F.I.R.E. Framework is cyclical and user-directed, which the circular blueprint captures beautifully. But stakeholders used to traditional formats might need the swimlane version for clarity.

When to use which:

  • Circular/Organic: Best for non-linear services, cyclical experiences, or emphasizing user autonomy
  • Swimlane: Best for stakeholder presentations, linear processes, or showing clear touchpoints
  • Journey Hybrid: Best when emotional experience is central, or combining UX + service design
  • Ecosystem Map: Best for showing relationships between system components, not sequence

Developing your style: Your "style" emerges from matching form to function. Ask: What's the core nature of this service? Is it linear or cyclical? Is the emotional journey the story? Let that guide your format choice.
